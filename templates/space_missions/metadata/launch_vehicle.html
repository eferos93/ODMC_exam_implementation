Vehicle metadata

  • Name: vehicle name, given by its manufacturer
  • Class: the vehicle type. It can be one of the following values:
    • Extraterrestrial Deep Space Launch
    • Missile
    • Orbital Vehicle
    • Research Rocket
    • Reentry Test Vehicle
    • Exoatmospheric test
    • Exoatmospheric weather rocket
    • Endoatmospheric missile
    • Cruise missile
    • Endoatmospheric reentry test vehicle
    • Endoatmospheric test/research
    • Endoatmospheric Weather rocket
  • Stages: set of stages that compose the vehicle.
    • Stage Name: the identifier of the stage
    • Stage Number: integer from -1 to 6, indicating the sequence of stages, or the value 'Fairing'
    • Dummy: binary value 'yes' or 'no' indicating if it is a dummy stage or not.
  • Manufacturer: manufacturer identifier.
  • Minimum number of stages: integer indicating the minimum number of stages supported by the vehicle
  • Maximum number of stages: integer indicating the maximum number of stages supported by the vehicle
  • Launch Mass: the mass at the moment of launch, expressed in tons.
  • Thrust: thrust expressed in kN
  • Length: length in meters.
