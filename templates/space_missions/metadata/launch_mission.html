Mission and Launch Metadata

  • Name: the mission's identifier.
  • Launch ID: the launch identifier.
  • Date: the launch date, expressed in the format Mon-DD-YYYY
  • Organisation: identifier of the organisation that organised the launch
    • {# TODO: add link to org metadata page #}
  • Vehicle: vehicle's identifier that refers to the Launch Vehicle table
  • Astronauts: list of astronauts that participated in this missions, together with the following data:
    • Full Name: the full name of the astronaut, more precisely "surname, name".
    • Role: string indicating the role of the astronaut in this particular mission.
    • Join Year: the year in which the astronaut joined the mission.
  • Launch type: the type of launch, the possible values are 'Orbital', 'DeepSpaceMission' and 'unknown'
  • Success or Fail: field that can assume two values, 'success' or 'fail', that indicates whether the mission/launch has been a success or failure
